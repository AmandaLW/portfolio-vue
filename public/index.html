PARK MY RIDE: PARKING MONITORING SYSTEM

This was my project for my senior capstone for my udergrad. My team consisted of two students, myself and my partner Alex Parenti. We developed a parking monitoring system that would take photos of a nearby parkining lot, determine if a car is present in each lot and send the information to an app. The majority of the software was developed with Python. We utalized two Google APIs, Google Vision to determine what is in each parking spot, and Google Firebase to send the parking availablity from the server to the app. We presented our work at the 26th anual University of Idaho Engineering Design EXPO and won "Best of Show".
